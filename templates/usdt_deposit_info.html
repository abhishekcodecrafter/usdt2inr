How it Works

  • Ensure you're depositing USDT on the TRC20 network.
  • Send USDT from any wallet to the provided deposit address.
  • Enter the transaction ID into the input field on our platform.
  • Our system will automatically verify the transaction.
  • Wait 5-10 minutes for confirmation, depending on network congestion.
  • Your wallet balance will be updated once the transaction is confirmed.
